About Me

Hello! I am Yang Luo (杨罗), currently a Ph.D. student affiliated with the Key Laboratory of Trustworthy Distributed Computing and Service (MoE) at the Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT), and a member of the Academician Fang Binxing Workstation.

My research interests primarily revolve around AI Security and Trustworthy AI Systems. Specifically, my recent work focuses on analyzing safety vulnerabilities and structural cognitive overload in Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs), investigating graph-based memory poisoning in AI agents (such as tool-use and mobile agents like miClaw), and designing robust defense mechanisms for end-to-end LLM ecosystems.

If you are interested in discussing collaboration or research regarding large model vulnerabilities and mobile AI agent defense, feel free to drop me an email!
